A Close-Knit Team of Seasoned Logistics Experts
Since 2016, PrimeTime continues to strive for excellence by providing excellent customer service and business partnership with US carriers and shippers. Our goal is simple: to increase your profitability by running efficient and effective analytical data.
Beyond contractual pricing in the spot market, we also act as an “extension” to your business and logistics department. In delivering our mission, PrimeTime equips its professionals from varying areas of expertise with the right knowledge and techniques to become highly skilled logisticians.
