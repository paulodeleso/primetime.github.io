About Us
COMPANY HISTORY
Where It All Began
PrimeTime Global Logistics was established by Robert Williams, an entrepreneur by heart with a passion for providing visibility and transparency to customers. Following his years of experience in Third Party Logistics, Robert envisioned great success in all aspects of transportation.

Beyond providing for his family and himself, he found an opportunity to also improve the lives of those who worked with him; these people included truck drivers, global representatives and other businesses seeking competitive shipping solutions.

Robert followed his goals and entrepreneurial spirit. In 2016, he explored the GTZ technology and services of GlobalTranz, which matched his current book of business and paved the way for opening his own agency. Fundamental training and tools were essentially provided for Robert to get started with his business.
It was a one-man show throughout the first year. With grit and determination, however, PrimeTime Global Logistics steadily grew its network and exponentially surpassed numbers in revenue and recruitment. Today, PrimeTime Global Logistics consists of three (3) main Departments, namely, Operations, Business Development, and Marketing. Each Department is supervised by the Management Team comprising Robert Williams, himself, Chris Kavanagh (Managing Partner) and Jonathan Meyer (General Manager).

While presently headquartered at West Palm Beach, Florida, PrimeTime Global Logistics employs remote functional teams across South America, Philippines and Eastern Europe.
