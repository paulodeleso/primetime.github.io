PRIMETIME GLOBAL LOGISTICS
Our Core Values
PrimeTime Global Logistics upholds a commendable code of ethics for its carrier partnership and customer service by providing quality shipping solutions to your business. Since 2016, we have devoted ourselves to bringing transparency within the 3PL industry. In line with this objective, we aim to build trust and help value customers gain a vision of us as their logistics ‘extension.’ Whether coast to coast or dock to dock shipping, it is our responsibility to designate a flexible and trustworthy operations team who will increase your overall profitability.
PRESTIGE
We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellent customer service using transparent and innovative shipping solutions.
RECOGNITION
We cultivate individual development and organizational growth by acknowledging hard work and rewarding milestones.
INTEGRITY
We enshrine teamwork with honesty and respect for members, stakeholders and business partners of the company.
MOMENTUM
We take accountability for our actions and make every effort to overcome any challenges or potential roadblocks.
EMPOWERMENT
We strongly believe that quality service starts by establishing trust and support towards carriers, representatives, and shippers.
